User Manual

Overview

NeuroGrid is a battery-powered ESP32-based cognitive training device using a 3×3 LED grid. It challenges users to memorize and reproduce LED sequences across three built-in modes.

Getting Started

1. Power on NeuroGrid. The LED will stabilize once it's ready.
2. On your phone, connect to Wi-Fi network NeuroGrid_AP.
3. Open your browser and go to 192.168.4.1.
4. The device starts in Normal Mode by default.

Modes Reference

Normal — Progressive memory game. Sequences grow longer over time.

Reverse — Same as Normal but you must recall the sequence in reverse order.

Speed — Rapid LED flashes to test raw reaction time.

Battery & Charging

NeuroGrid runs on a 2500mAh Li-ion battery. To charge, connect via a standard USB cable — the TP4056 module handles safe charging and over-discharge protection. After 15 seconds of inactivity, the device enters sleep mode automatically. Press any button to wake it.

Troubleshooting

If this page fails to load, ensure you are connected to NeuroGrid_AP and not your home network. If unresponsive, power cycle the device and wait 5–10 seconds before reconnecting.

⚠️ Do not expose to moisture or high heat. Ensure stable power during operation.
